Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Description

Spaghetti Aglio e Olio is an incredibly simple and incredibly delicious pasta recipe. This traditioanl Italian plate can be prepared by anyone for a perfect meal in a short amount of time.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. of dried spaghetti
  • ½ cup of olive oil
  • 6 to 8 cloves of sliced garlic
  • ½ cup of crushed red pepper flakes. (modify to taste)
  • (Optional) Chopped fresh parsley
  • (Optional) Grated parmesan

Preparation

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add spaghetti and cook until almost al dente (yes, it is important that it's not fully cooked yet)
  2. About 3 minutes after you added the pasta to the boiling water, heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Once hot, add the garlic and red pepper flakes and sauté for 3-5 minutes until the garlic is golden.
    • If the garlic is done before the pasta is ready, remove the pan from the heat. Do this to avoid burning the garlic.
  3. Once the pasta is ready, transfer the pasta into the sauté pan, along with ½ cup of pasta water.
  4. Toss the pasta continously until its evenly coated by the garlic sauce. Add a bit more pasta water if the sauce is too dry.
  5. Add any extra salt/red pepper flakes to taste and garnish with parsley/parmesan. Enjoy!
